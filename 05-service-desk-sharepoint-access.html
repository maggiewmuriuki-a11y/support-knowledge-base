Impact: High — unable to access SharePoint for urgent report.

🛠️ Investigation Steps

Step 1: Verify Identity & Account

  • Identity verified
  • Account active, MFA enabled
  • Sign-in logs confirm AADSTS53003 error

Step 2: Review Sign-In Logs

  • Error: AADSTS53003
  • Reason: Device not compliant
  • Policy: Require compliant device for SharePoint

Step 3: Confirm Policy Scope

  • Devices must be Entra ID joined
  • Devices must be compliant (Intune)
  • User device not enrolled

Step 4: Workaround Options

  • Option A: Use company laptop (selected)
  • Option B: Temporary browser exception

Step 5: Confirm Access

  • User switched to company device
  • Device compliant
  • Access successful

Step 6: Long-Term Recommendation

  • Consider enrolling personal devices in Intune

✅ Resolution

Summary: Access restored using compliant company device within 2 hours 51 minutes.

Root Cause: Conditional Access policy blocking non-compliant personal devices.
